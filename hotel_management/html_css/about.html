Sajek Hill View Resort, equipped with all modern state of the art amenities and facilities, located in among the hills of Kasalong range of mountains in Sajek union, Ruilui Para, Sajek Rangamati, Chittagong, Bangladesh in Rangamati District. The valley is 1800 feet above sea level. Sajek valley is known as the Queen of Hills & Roof of Rangamati. This resort is the true combination of ultimate luxury, gracious hospitality and admirable greenery. Classified with 08 rooms and suites to meet all your expectations.
